Instruction: Choose the word that is nearly opposite in meaning to the underlined word in each of the following sentences.

1: The old man occupied the rat-infested building in the slum.

A. Built
B. Inhabited
C. Rented
D. Vacated
The opposite of occupied is vacant of correct because, the term "occupied" means filled or taken while,vacated means to leave or abandon a place making it empty or unoccupied. Example: they were asked to vacate the building. So the correct answer is D. Vacated

2: The Auditor could not identify the authentic receipt.

A. Real
B. Fake
C. Earlier
D. Lost
The correct answer is B. Fake

3: The meat is very tough.?

C. Mild
D. Tender
The correct answer is D. Tender

4: Ojo denied that he stole the watch.

A. Admitted
B. Argued

D. Said
