4. Road accidents in Nigeria are now on the increase. This ugly situation should be checked because many lives are being lost yearly without caution. The question is - what are the causes and possible remedies? One of the chief causes is over— speeding by drivers. Many drivers speed without thinking of the possible outcome. This, without doubt, causes road accident. Secondly, many drivers have faulty brakes. They do not spare time to repair their faulty vehicles but would like to manage them. They do not have time to repair their vehicles. They want to get rich at all costs. This becomes an ill-wind blowing no good to anybody. Another cause of road accident is the presence of Police checkpoints on roads. This becomes more disadvantageous than what it had originally been intended for. In a bid to dodge the checkpoints, drivers speed so much that they are involved in fatal road accidents. From the foregoing, it is pertinent to suggest the following solutions to help to minimize road accidents, if not eradicate them. The first solution is that drivers should not over speed. The adage better be late than the late should be drummed into their ears. Lastly, vehicles should be checked always and maintained all the time to prevent loss of lives as a result of road accidents.
