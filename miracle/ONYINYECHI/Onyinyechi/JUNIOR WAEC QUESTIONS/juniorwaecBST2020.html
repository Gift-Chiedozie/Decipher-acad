1a. Uses of Ceramics

1. They are used in making potteries (such as plates, cup and saucer, .flower vase, pot, vessels)

2. They are used to produce whitewares (such as lavatory sinks and toilets, crucibles, sparkplug, dishes, and electrical insulators)

3. They are used to make tablewares (such as plates, glasses, bowls, mugs, utensils, dinnerware)

4. Ceramics are used to make tiles.

5. They are used to produce stone wares, which are mainly used to construct sewage pipes, underground cable sheathings, drainage pipes, valves and pumps in chemical industries.

6. They are used to make abrasives, which are mainly used for polishing, grinding, surface finishing and cutting operations.

Uses of Plastics

1. They are used for making dinnerware (dishes).

2. They are used for making electric switchboxes and switches.

3. They are used for making trays, cutlery handles, brushes and babydishes.

4. They are used for making funnels, radio and television cabinets.

5. They are used for making mobile phone casing, automobile accessories and spectacle (eye-glass) frames.

6. They are used for roofing sheets, refrigerator doors and air-conditioning housing.

7. They are used for making electric iron and cooking pot handles.

8. They are used for containers for liquids, computer housing cases, ball-point pen casing.

Uses of Rubber
