Section B: Basic Science

3a. Define growth?

Growth is defined as the increase in size of an organism. It is the process by which organisms increase in size and develop their mature form and function.

b. State three characteristic features of adulthood stage

1. During early adulthood, the Individual becomes fully developed physically, mentally and emotionally.

2. During middle adulthood, both men and women starts showing signs of decline or negative growth physically such as development of wrinkles, gray hair, and gaining weight. Around this stage, menopause (the period of time when a woman is no longer menstruating) may also occur in women.

3. During old age, wrinkles on the skin get more noticeable. Also, physical strength, sharpness in smell, taste, hearing, and vision decrease as well. In later stages of old age, there may be memory loss (the individual starts forgetting things easily).

Section D: Physical and Health Education

7a. What is healthful school living?

Healthful school living is a term used to describe that aspect of the school health program which is concerned with provision of a safe and healthful environment, the organization of a healthful school day, and the establishment of interpersonal relationships favourable to emotional, social, and physical health. OR

Healthful school living is the physical and aesthetic surrounding, psychosocial climate and culture of the school community.

b. What is school health service?

School health services refer to the health care delivery system that is operational within a school or college. These services aim at promoting and maintaining the health of the school students, teachers and other workers. OR

School health services are services from medical, teaching and other professionals applied in or out of school to improve the health and well-being of students and staff in the school.
